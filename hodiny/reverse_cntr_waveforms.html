Sample behavioral waveforms for design file "reverse_cntr.vhd"

The following waveforms show the behavior of lpm_counter megafunction for the chosen set of parameters in design "reverse_cntr.vhd". The design "reverse_cntr.vhd" is a 4 bit down modulus 10 counter. The counter supports an asynchronous load of data input.

Fig. 1 : Wave showing counter operation.

The output port cout will be asserted at the completion of count sequence. The ports cin and cout are used to chain multiple counters together.
